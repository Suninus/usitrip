Hotel Pickups
  We provide round trip transfers via motorcoach to our Executive Air Terminal at McCarran Int'l Airport from approximately 100 Las Vegas area properties, which includes all major Las Vegas area Resorts and Lodging.

Travel times to McCarran Int'l Airport vary depending on the proximity of the property you will be staying at. Each property has been assigned a Zone Number.
?/td>

For example, if the property you are staying at is located in Zone No. 1 (furthest from McCarran), total travel time to our Air Terminal and passenger check-in will take approximately 1 hour and 15 minutes. As compared to a property in Zone No. 8 (closest to McCarran), with a total transfer time of only 35 minutes. All times are approximate and will be confirmed prior to your departure.
