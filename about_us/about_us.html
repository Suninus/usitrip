About Usitrip.com
Usitrip.com (http://www.usitrip.com/) by UnitedStars International Ltd, as one of the world's leading online travel booking platforms, is applying avant-garde technology to offer a variety of world-class travel services and provide instant site search engines for group-buying deals , intelligent-booking tour packages, flights, study abroad, college study tours and other travel-related information to worldwide travelers. In addition to our intelligent Chinese-language booking system, we also provide customizable travel services through our experienced bilingual travel consultants.

Usitrip.com's headquarter is located in Los Angeles, California (Los Angeles County), which has a legal American Business License and California Seller of Travel License (CST#2110393-40-the registration number can be verified through the State of California). Usitrip.com maintains an A+ grade, which is the highest rating from the Better Business Bureau (BBB). We are the first and only Chinese-language online travel agency that has been certified A+ credibility by the BBB.

Since its founding in 2004, Usitrip.com has built strategic partnerships with China Tr avel Service Head Office Co., Ltd, China International Travel Service Ltd., Shenzhen Tourism International Co., Ltd., and Embassy of the United States, American Center. Based on servicing Chinese abroad and at home and anchored by its own high quality business credibility as well as professional service, it has provided excellent international tour services to over hundreds of thousands of customers. Furthermore, it earned Usitrip.com an honor of being the most famous and designated Chinese travel agency in the United States.
